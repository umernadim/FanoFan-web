Stand
Fans
Stand fans are tall, slender fans designed to save space while providing effective cooling and air circulation. These fans are typically designed with a vertical column structure, housing multiple small blades or impellers that generate airflow across a wide area.
Ceiling
Fans
Ceiling fans are mechanical devices mounted to the ceiling that help circulate air throughout a room. They consist of rotating blades that, when in motion, create a breeze that helps cool down a space by enhancing air movement.
Table
Fans
Table fans are small, portable fans designed to be placed on a table, countertop, or floor. They consist of rotating blades that generate airflow, providing focused and direct cooling to a specific area or individual.
Exhaust
Fans
Exhaust fans are ventilation devices installed in spaces like kitchens, bathrooms, basements, or laundry rooms, designed to expel air from a specific area to the outside environment.
