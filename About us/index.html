An electronic fan is a mechanical device that uses an electric motor to rotate blades, creating airflow to cool and ventilate indoor and outdoor spaces. Fans are essential appliances in homes, offices, and various industrial settings, providing relief from heat, improving air quality, and enhancing comfort.

Benefits

Energy Efficiency: Consume less energy compared to air conditioning, reducing electricity costs. Cooling Relief: Provide effective cooling during hot weather, enhancing comfort in various settings Portability: Lightweight and easy to move, allowing for flexible placement and airflow direction.The primary energy saving derives from thermal comfort benefits, keeping occupants comfortable at higher temperatures and allowing for increased cooling setpoints. In cooling, a room with fans is thermally comfortable over a wider range of temperatures than a room without fans.
